Nuestra Historia

Two decades of fire, flavor & familia.

La Flama has served Rock Island and the greater Quad Cities since 2004 — locally owned and proudly part of the community for over two decades. We bring elevated Mexican cuisine that honors traditional roots while cooking with non-saturated oils for a healthier approach that never sacrifices flavor.

Our all-day breakfast and vibrant lunch are made for nearby offices and professionals, served in an elegant, refined-but-welcoming dining room with warm reclaimed-wood textures. In December 2024 we opened our new location — same fresh ingredients, same bold, fiery taste.

Signature Plates

Fired up, made fresh, plated with pride.

01

Birria Tacos

Slow-braised, deeply spiced shredded beef on griddled corn tortillas with a rich consommé for dipping. "Sooo yummy," in the words of a regular — and we agree.

02

Street Tacos

Soft corn tortillas, cilantro, onion and a house salsa with a nice little kick — finished with our craveable salsa verde. A trio that earns the trip.

03

The Big Burrito

Our Wednesday special. "I unwrap it and it just melts in your mouth." Griddled golden, generously stuffed — steak, carnitas, al pastor, birria and more.

04

All-Day Breakfast

Huevos rancheros, fluffy waffles, loaded omelets and more — served from open to close. Breakfast all day, every day, just the way our regulars like it.
